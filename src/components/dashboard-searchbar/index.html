12 Things to Do in London

1. Visit the Tower of London

Explore the rich history of this iconic fortress and see the Crown Jewels.

2. Stroll Through Hyde Park

Enjoy a relaxing walk or boat ride in one of London’s largest parks.

3. Explore the British Museum

Discover ancient artifacts and art from around the world.

4. See Big Ben and the Houses of Parliament

Marvel at London’s iconic landmarks along the River Thames.

5. Take a Ride on the London Eye

Get panoramic views of the city from this giant observation wheel.

6. Wander Through Camden Market

Shop for unique items, street food, and vintage finds.

7. Visit Buckingham Palace

Watch the Changing of the Guard and explore royal history.

8. Explore Covent Garden

Experience street performances, shops, and dining in this vibrant area.

9. Tour the Tate Modern

View contemporary art exhibits in this renowned gallery.

10. Walk Along the South Bank

Enjoy views, entertainment, and cultural attractions along the Thames.

11. Visit the Natural History Museum

See fascinating exhibits about dinosaurs, geology, and more.

12. Explore Notting Hill

Wander through colorful streets, boutiques, and the famous Portobello Road Market.
